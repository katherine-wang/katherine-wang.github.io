about me
On campus, I am involved in social entrepreneurship as a 2012-2013 Fellow and later Internal Director of the UC Berkeley chapter of Compass Fellowship, an organization that provides the framework for freshman fellows to create a business plan for their own socially-minded start-up in just one year. I am also Managing Editor and Layout Editor of hardboiled, the Asian Pacific American newsmagazine at UC Berkeley and an active member of the progressive Asian Pacific Islander (API) community on campus. In the past, I served as Logistics Committee Co-Chair and Arts & Media Committee Member of the 2014 Asian Pacific Islanders Issues Conference (APIICON) Planning Team. I have also mentored in Cal Vietnamese Student Association's VISION program for the past three years. I hope to pursue a degree in law in the future.
